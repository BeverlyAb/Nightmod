(vertical children & options)

Returns an entity based on VerticalGroup.

(vertical [entity-1 entity-2])
Options
:align^int align
Sets the alignment of widgets within the vertical group. Set to {@link Align#center}, {@link Align#top}, {@link Align#bottom}, {@link Align#left}, {@link Align#right}, or any combination of those.
:center
Sets the alignment of widgets within the vertical group to {@link Align#center}. This clears any other alignment.
:fill
:fill^float fill
:get-align
:get-fill
:get-pad-bottom
:get-pad-left
:get-pad-right
:get-pad-top
:get-pref-height
:get-pref-width
:get-reverse
:get-space
:invalidate
:layout
:left
Sets {@link Align#left} and clears {@link Align#right} for the alignment of widgets within the vertical group.
:pad^float pad
Sets the padTop, padLeft, padBottom, and padRight to the specified value.
:pad^float top, ^float left, ^float bottom, ^float right
:pad-bottom^float pad-bottom
:pad-left^float pad-left
:pad-right^float pad-right
:pad-top^float pad-top
:reverse
The children will be ordered from bottom to top rather than the default top to bottom.
:reverse^boolean reverse
If true, the children will be ordered from bottom to top rather than the default top to bottom.
:right
Sets {@link Align#right} and clears {@link Align#left} for the alignment of widgets within the vertical group.
:set-round^boolean round
If true (the default), positions and sizes are rounded to integers.
:space^float spacing
Sets the space between children.
Source
(defn vertical*
  [children]
  (create-group (VerticalGroup.) children))
(defmacro vertical
  [children & options]
  `(let [entity# (vertical* ~children)]
     (u/calls! ^VerticalGroup (u/get-obj entity# :object) ~@options)
     entity#))
