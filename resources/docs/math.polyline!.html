(polyline! object k & options)

Calls a single method on a polyline.

Options
:calculate-length
:calculate-scaled-length
:dirty
:get-length
Returns the euclidean length of the polyline without scaling
:get-origin-x
:get-origin-y
:get-rotation
:get-scale-x
:get-scale-y
:get-scaled-length
Returns the euclidean length of the polyline
:get-transformed-vertices
Returns vertices scaled, rotated, and offset by the polygon position.
:get-vertices
Returns vertices without scaling or rotation and without being offset by the polyline position.
:get-x
:get-y
:rotate^float degrees
:scale^float amount
:set-origin^float origin-x, ^float origin-y
:set-position^float x, ^float y
:set-rotation^float degrees
:set-scale^float scale-x, ^float scale-y
:translate^float x, ^float y
