(ear-clipping-triangulator & options)

Returns an EarClippingTriangulator.

Options
:compute-triangles^FloatArray vertices

:compute-triangles^float[] vertices, ^int offset, ^int count
Triangulates the given (convex or concave) simple polygon to a list of triangle vertices.
