(rigid-body info & options)

Returns a body based on btRigidBody.

Source
(defn rigid-body*
  [info]
  (assoc (Body3D. (btRigidBody. info))
         :info info))
(defmacro rigid-body
  [info & options]
  `(let [body# (rigid-body* ~info)
         ^btRigidBody object# (:object body#)]
     (u/calls! object# ~@options)
     body#))
