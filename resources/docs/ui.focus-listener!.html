(focus-listener! screen k & options)

Calls a single method on the FocusListener in the screen.

Options
:handle^Event event
:keyboard-focus-changed^FocusListener.FocusEvent event, ^Actor actor, ^boolean focused
:scroll-focus-changed^FocusListener.FocusEvent event, ^Actor actor, ^boolean focused
Source
(defmacro focus-listener!
  [screen k & options]
  `(let [listeners# (u/get-obj ~screen :ui-listeners)
         ^FocusListener object# (u/get-obj listeners# :focus-listener)]
     (u/call! object# ~k ~@options)))
