(step! screen)
(step! screen entities)

Runs the physics simulations for a single frame and optionally returns the entities with their positions updated.

Source
(defn step!
  ([{:keys [delta-time max-sub-steps time-step]
     :or {max-sub-steps 5 time-step (/ 1 60)}
     :as screen}]
    (bullet-3d! screen :step-simulation delta-time max-sub-steps time-step))
  ([screen entities]
    (step! screen)
    (map (fn [e]
           (if (:body e)
             (assoc e
                    :x (body-x e)
                    :y (body-y e)
                    :z (body-z e))
             e))
         entities)))
